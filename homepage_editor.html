About Us

Thuto Keya Rona Tutoring And Projects was started in 2015, the organization provides after-school lessons, matric re-write and upgrade, access to government information and also relating to unemployment, health, arts and culture, Bana Pele and Batho Pele principles.

The After-School Program was established for the purpose of creating high-quality after-school programs outside of regular school hours. After-school programs are defined as programs that take place when school is not in session and provide educational enrichment and recreational activities for students in grades R-12 and have a parent involvement component. After-school programs provide students with academic enrichment opportunities, as well as additional activities designed to complement the Department of Basic Education’s academic programs. Services include tutoring and mentoring, homework help, academic enrichment (such as hands-on science or technology programs), community service opportunities, as well as music, arts, sports and cultural activities. At the same time, programs assist working parents by providing a safe environment for students.

Thuto keya rona has partnered with private colleges and institutions to offer the matric re-write and upgrade as well as skills development programmes. The arm of the partnership is to give access to quality education and also quality skills at an affordable prices.
